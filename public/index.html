Introducing 2026 Major tournament!

Welcome to the ultimate competitive battleground where strategy, skill, and teamwork collide the Round of 32 tournaments for Valorant, CS:GO, and Rainbow Six Siege are officially live! This stage marks the beginning of high-stakes action as 32 elite teams in each game enter the bracket to fight for glory and a chance to become champions. In Valorant, expect intense tactical plays, precise aim, and explosive clutches as teams battle across iconic maps like Ascent and Lotus. In Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO), legendary lineups return to prove their dominance with calculated utility usage and lightning-fast executions. And in Rainbow Six Siege, players must rely on sharp communication and clever operator strategy to breach, defend, and adapt under pressure. All three tournaments follow a single-elimination format meaning one mistake can send you home. The passion, pressure, and prestige of these matches are unmatched. Whether you're cheering for your favorite team or watching the rising stars of the competitive scene, this is the perfect time to dive in and witness the raw energy of esports. Follow the brackets, watch the highlights, and stay tuned because only the strongest will survive this round and move one step closer to the trophy.
